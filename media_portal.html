How we engage with the media

Study Group is a leading international educator and a trusted strategic partner to more than 50 universities around the world. We are committed to transparent and proactive engagement with the media to ensure accurate representation of our purpose in international education

Our media team provides expert insights and responds to coverage related to Study Group. For all media enquiries, please complete our contact form which will be forwarded to the team. Please include details of editorial deadlines to help us to ensure we provide a timely response. We aim to reply promptly and provide relevant information for accurate reporting.

Our commitment to external communications

We believe in open, responsible, and factual communication, particularly on issues impacting international students, higher education, and our partnerships with universities.

Our approach:

Transparency: We respond to media enquiries in a timely and open manner. Accuracy: We ensure all statements reflect the latest verified information and data. Advocacy: We champion the value of international education and actively address misconceptions.
