Our Story

Our Background

RNS Education Ltd. was established in London in 2019 with a clear purpose: to support domestic students across the United Kingdom in accessing the right educational opportunities through ethical, transparent, and student-focused guidance. From its inception, the organisation was built on the belief that informed decision-making and personalised support are essential to student success

In its early years, RNS Education focused on understanding the needs of local students and the evolving UK education landscape. By working closely with recognised institutions and maintaining a strong awareness of admission requirements, the company developed a reliable and structured approach to student recruitment and support. This commitment quickly led to high levels of student satisfaction and trust, with many students recommending RNS Education’s services to others.

We equip students to become the leaders and global citizens of tomorrow. Together, we create a better world through global education.
