Home > Media Portal > Statement regarding industrial action at Sheffield IC

Updated 25 February 2026

Following the outcome of the recent industrial action ballot at Sheffield International College, which resulted in a vote in favour of strike action, a series of discontinuous strike days are planned to take place on the following dates:

  • Wednesday 4th March
  • Friday 6th March
  • Tuesday 10th March
  • Thursday 12th March

We continue to engage to address current concerns raised through this recent ballot. Students will be contacted directly with information on the measures in place to support them and minimise any disruption to their education.
